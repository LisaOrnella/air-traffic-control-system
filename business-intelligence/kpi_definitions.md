# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
## Air Traffic Control System

## 1. SAFETY KPIs

### 1.1 Safety Compliance Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage of operations complying with safety protocols
- **Formula:** (Compliant Operations / Total Operations) × 100
- **Target:** 100%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Real-time
- **Data Source:** Audit logs, safety checks
- **Owner:** Safety Officer

### 1.2 Conflict Prevention Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage of potential runway conflicts successfully prevented
- **Formula:** (Prevented Conflicts / Total Potential Conflicts) × 100
- **Target:** 100%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Daily
- **Data Source:** Conflict detection logs
- **Owner:** Operations Manager

### 1.3 Emergency Response Time
- **Definition:** Average time from emergency report to runway clearance
- **Formula:** Σ(Response Times) / Number of Emergencies
- **Target:** < 5 minutes
- **Measurement Frequency:** Per incident
- **Data Source:** Emergency alerts timeline
- **Owner:** Emergency Coordinator

## 2. EFFICIENCY KPIs

### 2.1 On-time Performance (OTP)
- **Definition:** Percentage of flights arriving/departing within 15 minutes of schedule
- **Formula:** (On-time Flights / Total Flights) × 100
- **Target:** > 90%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Daily
- **Data Source:** Flight status records
- **Owner:** Operations Director

### 2.2 Runway Utilization Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage of available runway time actually used
- **Formula:** (Used Runway Time / Available Runway Time) × 100
- **Target:** 70-80%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Hourly
- **Data Source:** Assignment records
- **Owner:** Resource Manager

### 2.3 Gate Turnaround Time
- **Definition:** Average time between aircraft departure and next arrival at same gate
- **Formula:** Σ(Turnaround Times) / Number of Gate Uses
- **Target:** < 45 minutes
- **Measurement Frequency:** Daily
- **Data Source:** Gate assignment logs
- **Owner:** Gate Manager

## 3. OPERATIONAL KPIs

### 3.1 Flight Volume Capacity
- **Definition:** Number of flights handled vs. system capacity
- **Formula:** (Actual Flights / Maximum Capacity) × 100
- **Target:** 85-90%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Hourly
- **Data Source:** Flight scheduling system
- **Owner:** Capacity Planner

### 3.2 Resource Availability
- **Definition:** Percentage of time resources are available for use
- **Formula:** (Available Time / Total Time) × 100
- **Target:** > 95%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Real-time
- **Data Source:** Resource status monitoring
- **Owner:** Resource Coordinator

### 3.3 Weather Impact Index
- **Definition:** Measure of weather-related operational disruptions
- **Formula:** (Weather-affected Operations / Total Operations) × Severity Factor
- **Target:** < 10%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Daily
- **Data Source:** Weather and operations logs
- **Owner:** Weather Officer

## 4. QUALITY KPIs

### 4.1 Passenger Satisfaction Score
- **Definition:** Average rating from passenger feedback
- **Formula:** Σ(Passenger Ratings) / Number of Responses
- **Target:** > 4.5/5.0
- **Measurement Frequency:** Monthly
- **Data Source:** Passenger surveys
- **Owner:** Customer Service Manager

### 4.2 Airline Partner Satisfaction
- **Definition:** Satisfaction rating from airline partners
- **Formula:** Σ(Airline Ratings) / Number of Airlines
- **Target:** > 4.0/5.0
- **Measurement Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Data Source:** Partner surveys
- **Owner:** Partnership Manager

### 4.3 Staff Efficiency Ratio
- **Definition:** Operations handled per staff member
- **Formula:** Total Operations / Number of Staff
- **Target:** Industry benchmark +10%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Monthly
- **Data Source:** Staff and operations records
- **Owner:** HR Director

## 5. FINANCIAL KPIs

### 5.1 Operational Cost per Flight
- **Definition:** Average cost to handle each flight
- **Formula:** Total Operational Costs / Number of Flights
- **Target:** Reduce by 5% annually
- **Measurement Frequency:** Monthly
- **Data Source:** Financial and operations systems
- **Owner:** Finance Director

### 5.2 Revenue per Available Seat
- **Definition:** Revenue generated per available seat
- **Formula:** Total Revenue / Available Seats
- **Target:** Industry average +15%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Monthly
- **Data Source:** Financial and booking systems
- **Owner:** Revenue Manager

### 5.3 Return on Investment (ROI)
- **Definition:** Return from system investments
- **Formula:** (Gains from Investment - Cost of Investment) / Cost of Investment
- **Target:** > 20%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Annually
- **Data Source:** Financial reports
- **Owner:** CFO

## 6. INNOVATION KPIs

### 6.1 Automation Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage of operations automated
- **Formula:** (Automated Operations / Total Operations) × 100
- **Target:** Increase by 10% annually
- **Measurement Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Data Source:** System logs
- **Owner:** Innovation Manager

### 6.2 System Uptime
- **Definition:** Percentage of time system is operational
- **Formula:** (Operational Time / Total Time) × 100
- **Target:** 99.9%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Real-time
- **Data Source:** System monitoring
- **Owner:** IT Director

### 6.3 Feature Adoption Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage of users adopting new features
- **Formula:** (Users Adopting Feature / Total Users) × 100
- **Target:** > 80% within 3 months
- **Measurement Frequency:** Monthly
- **Data Source:** Usage analytics
- **Owner:** Product Manager

## 7. COMPLIANCE KPIs

### 7.1 Regulatory Compliance Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage compliance with aviation regulations
- **Formula:** (Compliant Items / Total Requirements) × 100
- **Target:** 100%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Data Source:** Compliance audits
- **Owner:** Compliance Officer

### 7.2 Audit Success Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage of passed audits
- **Formula:** (Passed Audits / Total Audits) × 100
- **Target:** 100%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Per audit
- **Data Source:** Audit reports
- **Owner:** Quality Manager

### 7.3 Incident Reporting Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage of incidents properly reported
- **Formula:** (Reported Incidents / Total Incidents) × 100
- **Target:** 100%
- **Measurement Frequency:** Monthly
- **Data Source:** Incident reports
- **Owner:** Safety Manager

## 8. SUSTAINABILITY KPIs

### 8.1 Carbon Efficiency
- **Definition:** Carbon emissions per flight operation
- **Formula:** Total Carbon Emissions / Number of Flights
- **Target:** Reduce by 3% annually
- **Measurement Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Data Source:** Environmental reports
- **Owner:** Sustainability Manager

### 8.2 Energy Consumption
- **Definition:** Energy used per operational hour
- **Formula:** Total Energy Used / Operational Hours
- **Target:** Reduce by 5% annually
- **Measurement Frequency:** Monthly
- **Data Source:** Utility reports
- **Owner:** Facilities Manager

### 8.3 Waste Reduction
- **Definition:** Percentage reduction in operational waste
- **Formula:** ((Previous Waste - Current Waste) / Previous Waste) × 100
- **Target:** 10% annual reduction
- **Measurement Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Data Source:** Waste management reports
- **Owner:** Operations Manager

## 9. KPI REPORTING FRAMEWORK

### 9.1 Reporting Schedule
- **Real-time:** Safety, Emergency, Availability
- **Daily:** OTP, Utilization, Volume
- **Weekly:** Trends, Comparisons
- **Monthly:** Financial, Satisfaction, Compliance
- **Quarterly:** Strategic, Innovation, Sustainability

### 9.2 Visualization Standards
- **Green:** On target (100-90%)
- **Yellow:** Warning (89-80%)
- **Red:** Critical (<80%)
- **Trend Arrows:** ▲ Improving, ▼ Declining, ➔ Stable

### 9.3 Data Quality Standards
- **Accuracy:** > 99.5%
- **Timeliness:** < 5 minute delay
- **Completeness:** > 98%
- **Consistency:** Across all reports
